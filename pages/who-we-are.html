Yi Pune Chapter

Young Indians (Yi) is a division of the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII), established to provide a platform for young business leaders, entrepreneurs, and professionals to contribute to the nation's growth. Founded in 2002, Yi has grown into a vibrant movement of over 8,000 members across 65+ chapters throughout India.Our platform brings together passionate individuals aged 25-40 years who share a common vision of creating a better India through innovation, leadership development, and social impact initiatives.

About CII

The Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) is a non-government, not-for-profit, industry-led and industry-managed organization, playing a proactive role in India's development process.

Founded in 1895, CII is India's premier business association with a direct membership of over 9,000 organizations from the private as well as public sectors, including SMEs and MNCs.

Yi operates as a division of CII, leveraging the parent organization's extensive network and resources to amplify the impact of young leaders across the country.
