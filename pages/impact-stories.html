Transforming Farming in Kolhapur

Yi Yuva Rural Initiative

"When Yi Pune came to our village, we were struggling with traditional farming methods and poor yields. Today, our cooperative has increased income by 300%."

The Challenge

Farmers in Kolhapur district were using outdated techniques, resulting in low productivity and financial distress. Over 200 farming families were considering abandoning agriculture.

Our Intervention

Yi Pune introduced modern farming techniques, formed farmer cooperatives, and facilitated direct market access through digital platforms.

Breaking Barriers to Education

In rural Maharashtra, 40% of girls drop out of school after 10th grade due to financial constraints and lack of digital literacy.

Digital Classrooms Initiative

Yi Pune established digital learning centers in 50 villages, providing free coaching, computer training, and career guidance to rural students.

Remarkable Results

85% reduction in dropout rates, 200+ students secured scholarships for higher education, and 100% improvement in digital literacy scores.

- Sunita Jadhav, Parent & Community Leader
