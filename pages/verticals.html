Our Verticals & Initiatives

Thirteen key areas driving leadership, innovation, inclusion, and nation building

Learning

Empowering Minds, Enabling Leadership

Objective

Learning is the foundation of transformation. Through curated knowledge forums, industry-led masterclasses, and leadership development platforms, this vertical empowers members to evolve as visionary entrepreneurs and responsible leaders. It fosters a culture of continuous growth, strategic thinking, and future-readiness.

Branding

Amplifying Identity, Showcasing Impact

Objective

Branding strengthens Yi's voice and presence. By leveraging strategic communication, digital outreach, and impactful storytelling, this vertical ensures that every initiative, achievement, and social impact reaches the right audience. It builds credibility, influence, and a powerful community identity.

Climate Change

Leading Sustainable Transformation

Objective

Climate Change is not just an initiative; it is a responsibility. This vertical drives awareness and action towards sustainability, ESG integration, carbon footprint reduction, and environmentally responsible practices. It aims to create businesses and communities that thrive in harmony with the planet.

Road Safety

Building a Culture of Responsibility

Objective

Road Safety promotes disciplined driving behavior, awareness of traffic regulations, and accident prevention initiatives. Through structured campaigns and collaborations, it strives to create safer cities and responsible citizens.

Innovation

Inspiring Ideas that Shape Tomorrow

Objective

Innovation fuels progress. This vertical encourages disruptive thinking, startup incubation, technology adoption, and solution-driven approaches. It creates platforms where ideas transform into impactful enterprises and sustainable business models.

Health

Strengthening Well-being, Strengthening Communities

Objective

Health focuses on holistic well-being - physical, mental, and social. From health camps and blood donation drives to mental wellness awareness, this vertical ensures that strong individuals build stronger communities.

Entrepreneurship

Creating Wealth, Generating Impact

Objective

Entrepreneurship is at the heart of Yi. This vertical nurtures business leaders by offering mentorship, networking, funding guidance, and growth strategies. It empowers entrepreneurs to create employment, drive economic development, and contribute meaningfully to society.

Sports

Discipline, Teamwork, and Resilience

Objective

Sports promotes discipline, teamwork, and resilience. Through tournaments and sporting engagements, it strengthens bonds within the community while encouraging a healthy and competitive spirit.

Membership

Building a Strong Ecosystem of Trust and Collaboration

Objective

Membership is the backbone of Yi. It ensures meaningful engagement, value-driven participation, leadership opportunities, and a strong ecosystem of collaboration and trust.

She Shines

A Movement Towards Inclusive Leadership

Objective

She Shines is a movement towards inclusive leadership. It creates platforms for women entrepreneurs and professionals to rise, lead, and inspire. Through mentorship and visibility, it accelerates women-led growth and impact.

Rural

Inclusive Growth for Underserved Communities

Objective

The Rural initiative focuses on uplifting underserved communities through education, skill development, livelihood enhancement, and infrastructure support. It aims to bridge the urban-rural gap and foster inclusive growth.

YUVA

Nurturing Young Ethical Leaders

Objective

YUVA engages college students to instill leadership, entrepreneurial thinking, and social responsibility. It serves as a launchpad for young minds to become ethical leaders and changemakers.

Thalir

Planting Leadership at the School Level

Objective

Thalir plants the seeds of leadership at the school level. By instilling civic responsibility, integrity, and confidence among students, it prepares the next generation to build a stronger nation.
