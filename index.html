About Young Indians Pune

Young Indians (Yi) Pune is a movement of inspired young leaders contributing to nation building through innovation, entrepreneurship, and social impact initiatives. As part of the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII), we bring together passionate professionals aged 25-40 to create meaningful change in our communities and beyond.
